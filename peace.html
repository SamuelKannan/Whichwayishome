Peace

Peace is the harmony, and calmness that supersedes earthly circumstances or in other words, does not mean to be in a place where there is no noise, trouble or hardwork. It means to be in the midst of those things and still be calm in your heart. So we see peace is a gift of God.

  • 'Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives. Do not let your hearts be troubled and do not be afraid.' - John 14:27
    Meditation: So we see that peace is a gift that only Jesus can give

  • 'This is what the Lord says— your Redeemer, the Holy One of Israel: “I am the Lord your God, who teaches you what is best for you, who directs you in the way you should go. If only you had paid attention to my commands, your peace would have been like a river, your well-being like the waves of the sea.' - Isaiah 48:17-18
