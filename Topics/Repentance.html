Repentance

Remorse over sin that produces a profound change of heart, mind and actions moving away from wrongdoing and towards a renewed God-honoring life. So often it seems that the idea of repentance puts a bad flavor in people's mouth, when in reality it is one of the most amazing things someone could ever experience. Thomas Carlyle said, "Of all acts of man repentance is the most divine. The greatest of all faults is to be conscious of none."
