Sexuality

Sexuality, ahh it seems to be a hot topic these days. In a world full of thousands of differing opinions, God makes clear His own. And you know what, they didn't match mine and they probably won't match yours. But the Creator of it has the right to say what it is and what it is not; how to use it and how not to.
