Depression

What is it? you tell me? For "Each heart knows its own bitterness..." (Proverbs 14:10)

  • "Why, my soul, are you downcast? Why so disturbed within me?..."- Psalms 42:5
    Why are you depressed?
    Those who struggle with depression are those who have lost sight of all hope! But there is still hope, the rest of Psalms 42:5 offers the cure to depression.
    "...Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise him, my Savior and my God."

  • "Bitterly she weeps at night, tears are on her cheeks. Among all her lovers there is no one to comfort her. All her friends have betrayed her; they have become her enemies."- Lamentations 1:2
    Meditation: There is comfort to be found -
    "Praise be to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of compassion and the God of all comfort, who comforts us in all our troubles...".

  • So with you: Now is your time of grief, but I will see you again and you will rejoice, and no one will take away your joy. - John 16:22

  • You make known to me the path of life; you will fill me with joy in your presence, with eternal pleasures at your right hand. - Psalms 16:11
    Meditation: Joy is found in the presence of Jesus
