Self Worth

Where does my worth come from?

is it in strength? beauty? intellect? athleticism? money?. Is your worth rooted in any of these? If it is, it's a fleeting worth. A very fragile worth indeed. One car accident can remove everyone of these identities in an instant. Then what value would you be?
No, thanks to God, our worth is one declared outside of ourseleves, independent of humanity, and secure far beyond the reaches of the status quo. Your worth is one declared and set by your Creator, the One who made you.
And He began making your worth known when after five days of speaking all of creation into being. He paused to take time on you, humanity. He formed us with our own hands, made us in His own image, gave life in His own breath, and when His justice called for our due punishment, He redeemed us with His own blood. A beautiful statement of worth, I would say!.
That statement wasn't made because we are special, valuable or worthy of it. Our value has nothing to do us and quite everything to do with him. He loves the unlovable, forgives the unforgiveable, values the unvaluable, declares worth over the unworthy. You see our worth is established by something outside of ourselves and completley unreliant on ourselves and anybody else. Our worth is determined and held secure by the indesturible being that sustains all life. His price tag on us is one of unfathomable cost. The problem is do we know it?
