Peace

It does not mean to be in a place where there is no noise, trouble or hardwork. It means to be in the midst of those things and still be calm in your heart.

  • 'Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives. Do not let your hearts be troubled and do not be afraid.' - John 14:27

    'Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus. ' - Philippians 4:6-7

    Meditation: Jesus gives peace. His peace surpassess the ability to understand because it withstands the floods of awful circumstance. When others despair you will stay strong because of God's true peace. It's a supernatural peace that lasts unconditionally forever.
    Want it? Invite Jesus into your problem.

  • 'In peace I will lie down and sleep, for you alone, Lord , make me dwell in safety.' - Psalms 4:8
