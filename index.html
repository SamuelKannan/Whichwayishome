Which Way is Home?

Home

Home the place where you belong the place where we all desire to feel the most comfortable loved and protected. You may have never experienced this warmth before but I guarantee you wanted it. Its not too late, God's arms are still open and within His embrace you will find home.

The hole He has in His heart for you can never be replaced by another, the hole you have in your heart is for him and can never be filled by eating, drinking, sex and anything in life that keeps us distracted from the reality that life itself is just a moment. You are here and gone and your name and place will be forgotten. You are but one grain of sand in the hourglass of time. Will you accept the way home before it's too late? Jesus said "I'm the way." John 14:6
